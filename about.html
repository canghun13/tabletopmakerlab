About the lab

A workbench for the work behind the game.

Tabletop Maker Lab makes practical planning tools for board game designers, tabletop creators, and indie publishers.

Creator-first, not player-first

This is not a generic board-game site. It is for the questions that arrive when you are specifying a prototype, reading a factory quote, pricing a campaign, or assessing a publishing offer.

Useful inputs, transparent limits

We do not invent real-time manufacturing prices or hide decisions behind a black box. Where a number depends on your project, you should be able to bring your own quote, dimensions, assumptions, and scenario.

Built to grow carefully

The library starts with a small set of anchor tools. Each should be clear, useful, and supported by the guides and reference material needed to use it responsibly.
