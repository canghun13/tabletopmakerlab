How the comparison works

Total cost equals manufacturing subtotal plus tooling, inspection, other fixed costs, freight, duties, customs, inbound, and other per-unit costs multiplied by quantity.

Input guidance

Enter only values supplied or approved for your project, in one currency. If production quantities differ, the tool warns you because a per-game comparison becomes more meaningful.

Example

A lower factory subtotal can lose its advantage when tooling or freight is higher. The table keeps those drivers visible rather than ranking only by a headline unit price.

Assumptions and limits

This tool does not estimate supplier prices, exchange rates, tax treatment, or shipping. Confirm commercial terms and final specifications with your suppliers.
