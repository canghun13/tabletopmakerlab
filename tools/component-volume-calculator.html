Why packing allowance matters

Geometric volume assumes pieces tile perfectly. Components rarely do: discs roll, irregular shapes bridge gaps, and players need room to sort and remove pieces.

Method

Cuboids use width × height × depth. Cylinders and discs use pi × radius squared × height. The allowance is applied only after quantity is calculated.

Example

Use a 20–35% allowance for loose tokens, then compare with a bag, tray, or compartment mock-up.

Limits

This calculator handles one component type at a time and is not an automated nesting engine.
