Method

Base freight per game is freight divided by shipment quantity. Total logistics per game includes every entered handling, delivery, insurance, brokerage, surcharge, and other amount.

Example

The same total shipment cost distributes very differently across 2,400 versus 3,600 games. The scenarios illustrate allocation only; they do not predict a carrier rate.

Limits

Enter a confirmed or deliberately assumed shipment cost. This calculator does not quote freight or account for dimensional-weight rules, routes, delays, or tax.
