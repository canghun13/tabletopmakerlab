Method

Contribution margin equals average pledge minus per-backer product, packaging, fulfillment, shipping subsidy, other variable costs, and entered percentage fees. Fixed campaign costs divided by positive contribution margin gives the break-even backer count.

Example and limits

Use a realistic average pledge rather than a headline tier. This is campaign economics, not a platform fee or tax calculator.
