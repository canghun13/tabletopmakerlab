Method

Total landed cost is manufacturing plus logistics/import costs plus tooling, QC, other fixed costs, and quantity times other per-unit costs. Cost per game divides that total by production quantity.

Example

For 3,000 copies, a freight number that looks small beside manufacturing can still move the per-game cost enough to change a pledge or wholesale margin decision.

Assumptions and limits

Enter actual project values in a single currency. Confirm tax treatment, Incoterms, dimensional charges, and final supplier invoices independently.
