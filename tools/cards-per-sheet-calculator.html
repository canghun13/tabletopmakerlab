Card yield changes when a sheet is rotated. This calculator checks both simple grid orientations and selects the better planning yield.

Method

Bleed is added to all four card edges; margin and gutters reduce usable sheet area. The result is a simple rectangle grid, not printer-ready imposition.

Example

Limits
