Publisher vs Self-Publishing Economics

Compare responsibility, risk, and economics without a universal winner.

Quick answer

A publisher deal can shift operations and inventory exposure; self-publishing carries more direct responsibility and potentially different margin structure. Compare actual rights, capital, timing, and capacity.
