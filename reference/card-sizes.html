Standard Board Game Card Sizes

Card format families and sleeve-fit planning.

Quick answer

Cards vary by manufacturer; verify width, height, corner radius, and sleeve fit with samples. Poker, bridge, mini, Euro, tarot, square, and custom are useful labels, not a universal specification.

Creator workflow

Capture the actual specification, quote, or platform setting for your project, then use the related calculation with those values rather than a generic market assumption.

Common mistakes

Do not treat a category label, supplier term, or platform policy as a universal product specification. Confirm the current version, territory, and contract terms that apply to you.
